Texture Filtering Visualizer

Controls

  • Click a pixel to inspect its texture footprint.
  • Click the same pixel again to deselect it.
  • Move triangle vertices to change projection and distortion.
  • Adjust vertex depth (W) to observe perspective correction.
  • Modify UV coordinates to change texture mapping.
  • Change texture resolution.
  • Change checker size.
  • Switch between interpolation modes.
  • Switch between filtering modes.
  • Adjust anisotropy level (1x–16x).
  • Switch visualization modes.
  • Use the stepper to advance rasterization pixel-by-pixel or row-by-row.
  • Select a mip level to inspect generated mipmaps.
